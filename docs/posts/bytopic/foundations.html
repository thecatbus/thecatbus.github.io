Over time, I have found that I learn best by explaining-- partly why this blog exists. These articles are on things I have tried to learn properly at some point, through coursework or otherwise. The articles present the subject in a form that made most sense to me, and are not guaranteed to be accurate representations of the topic. Importantly, I ask why any particular objects are natural things to think about, so contrary to a typical mathematical text, often the focus is on definitions rather than results. Nonetheless, there should be enough content in each article to start you off on a reading quest of your own.
